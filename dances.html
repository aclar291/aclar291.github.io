Popular Dances and Songs

My club away from club...

Because of its history, it makes sense that country music plays at line dancing, but you may be surprised to learn that there’s a lot of other genres that you can dance to at line dancing! Some songs may share the same dance, so when requesting a song with the DJ you might need to know what the dance is called. For example, the Cooler Than Me dance works for the song Womanizer by Britney Spears.

Rap, EDM, and Pop

This is the sound of the club in a country setting. Popular songs to dance to include:

  • Damn I Love Miami by Pitbull
  • Evacuate the Dancefloor by Cascada
  • Buttons by The Pussycat Dolls

Country

This is what comes to mind when you hear "line dancing". Still, the styles of songs are diverse:

  • A Bar Song (Tipsy) by Shaboozey
  • Save A Horse (Ride A Cowboy) by Big & Rich
  • Sin Wagon by The Chicks

Rock and Roll

My favorite genre makes an appearance at line dancing! You might hear:

  • What I Like About You by The Romantics
  • Kickstart My Heart by Mötley Crüe
  • Burn It to the Ground by Nickelback

Skill Levels

No matter your background in dance (or lack thereof) you will be able to dance.

There are 3 distinct levels of line dancing: Beginner, Intermediate, and Difficult.
